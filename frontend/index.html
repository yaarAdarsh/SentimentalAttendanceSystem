    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does the system identify individuals for attendance tracking?

    The system uses AI-powered facial recognition technology to capture and analyze facial features, ensuring accurate and automated identification.

    What emotions can the system detect?

    The system can detect a range of basic emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, surprise, and neutral expressions, depending on the implemented emotion analysis model.

    Is the data collected by the system secure?

    Yes, the system employs robust security measures, such as encrypted data storage and secure access controls, to protect sensitive information.

    Can this system be integrated with existing attendance management tools?

    Absolutely! The system can be customized to integrate with popular attendance management or HR systems for seamless operation.

    Does the system require special hardware?

    No, the system works with standard cameras and computers. However, for optimal performance, high-quality cameras are recommended for accurate recognition.
